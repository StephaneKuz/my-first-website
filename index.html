PROCESS

I have a robust process for delivering high quality websites. I work through a series of milestones that allows me to craft an impeccable finished product. These phases take every project from conception through to launch day. In simplified form, here’s how it works:

DISCOVERY

I start by getting to know your business, your product or service and audience. I research and analyze what other similar companies do. I also look at current design trends.

READ MORE

Once I have a deep understanding of the vision and goals for the project, I will carry out extensive research into similar sites, looking at their strength and weaknesses; in order to find out the best processes to implement and in order to help the business gain an edge in its current marketplace.

DESIGN

After deciding on the project's Identity, I dive right into the Design Phase. This involves creating wireframes and high-fidelity designs. I create designs that look great.

READ MORE

With the strategy in hand, I begin to develop the conceptual designs. I design any pages, elements and assets required for the project. I craft sleek websites that are both beautiful and technically compliant.

DEVELOP

Once the design is finished the website will be built. This involves taking the design and coding it so that it works across all different devices and screen sizes.

READ MORE

Once I’m ready to move ahead with the conceptual design work, I begin to build or develop the project. I take pride in flawlessly replicating the prepared designs. I build the new website to match the designs exactly. Every website that I create is responsive (also known as mobile and tablet friendly).

LAUNCH

I test, tweak, improve and refine, making sure every pixel is perfect. I test the website in all major browsers and on various platforms. When the website is ready I will put it live.

READ MORE

After adding all content, making requested changes and migrating files to the hosting, it's time to launch!

SKILLS

When you need someone with the following skills, I can help you:

PHOTOSHOP

Photoshop is what I use to design sites. I literally use Photoshop on a daily basis. From wireframes, to website mockups, to photo editing, I rely on Photoshop for so much.

HTML

HTML is the standard markup language for creating Web pages. With HTML, I can build web pages that contain text, images, and many other types of content.

CSS

CSS is a style sheet language that describes the presentation of web pages. CSS is what I use to style the HTML with colors, backgrounds, font sizes, layout and more.

JAVASCRIPT & JQUERY

Since I love JavaScript, I'm a great fan of jQuery, the most popular JavaScript Library. That’s what I use when I want to make web pages more interactive, interesting and usable.

RESPONSIVE WEBSITES

Your tablet or smartphone choice shouldn’t affect your browsing experience. All the websites that I build work across multiple screen sizes and device types.

FRONT END OPTIMIZATION

People love websites that load fast, and every second counts. I can modify a website specifically to increase the loading speed. And that makes a great user experience.

BROWSER COMPATIBILITY

Whether you’re into Google Chrome — the most popular web browser, Mozilla Firefox, Internet Explorer, Safari or Opera, I am committing to providing you with full functionality.
